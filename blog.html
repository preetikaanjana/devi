Understanding Women's Rights

1. Right to Education

Every girl has the right to receive an education. Education empowers women, enabling them to make informed choices about their lives and futures.

2. Right to Work

Women have the right to work and pursue careers. This right not only provides financial independence but also allows women to contribute to society.

3. Right to Health

Access to healthcare is a fundamental right for all women. This includes reproductive health services, maternal care, and mental health support.

4. Right to Inherit Property

Women have the right to inherit property from their parents and husbands. This legal recognition is crucial for ensuring financial independence and security.

5. Right to Make Choices

Women have the right to make choices about their own lives, including decisions about marriage, children, and careers. Every woman should have the freedom to choose her path.
